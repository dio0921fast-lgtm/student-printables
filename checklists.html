Printable checklists for school and study

Student checklists are useful when a task has several small steps. These pages help students prepare for exams, pack supplies, and make sure important school items are not forgotten.

Print a checklist before a test, shopping trip, new semester, or school reset. Students can check items off by hand and print a new copy whenever they need the same routine again.

When to use printable school checklists

Before exams and quizzes

Exam and study checklists help students review notes, practice sample questions, pack test supplies, and check off final preparation steps before the test day arrives.

For supplies and school resets

A school supplies checklist is useful before a new school year, semester, or class change. Students can mark what they already have and what still needs to be packed or purchased.

For repeatable routines

Printed checklists are helpful for routines that happen again and again. Keep a blank copy ready, then print a new one whenever a student needs a clean checklist.
