Printable checklists for school and study

Student checklists are useful when a task has several small steps. These pages help students prepare for exams, pack supplies, and make sure important school items are not forgotten.

Print a checklist before a test, shopping trip, new semester, or school reset. Students can check items off by hand and print a new copy whenever they need the same routine again.
