Class Notes Template

Free printable class notes template for students to capture lecture ideas, key vocabulary, questions, and next study steps.

What is included?

  • Course, date, and topic fields
  • Main notes section
  • Key terms and definitions area
  • Questions to ask after class
  • Follow-up study tasks checklist

How to use this notes template

  1. Write the class topic and date at the top.
  2. Capture the main ideas during the lesson.
  3. Add key terms, formulas, or definitions in the side section.
  4. List questions you want to review or ask your teacher.
  5. Turn follow-up items into study tasks after class.

Who this class notes template is for

This class notes template is for students who want a cleaner way to capture lecture notes, key terms, questions, and follow-up tasks. It is helpful for classroom lessons, online courses, tutoring, and study groups.

How students can use it

Students can write the main notes during class, pull out important vocabulary or formulas, and record questions to ask later. The follow-up section turns notes into next steps instead of leaving them as a loose page of information.

Tips for printing and reusing it

Print multiple copies and keep them by subject in a binder. Reviewing completed note pages before a test can make it easier to find weak spots and important topics.
