Who this dorm packing checklist printable is for

This dorm packing checklist printable is for students preparing for dorm move-in, apartment living, orientation week, or a new semester away from home.

How students can use this dorm packing checklist printable

Students can check off items by category, add missing supplies, and mark what has already been packed. The list helps avoid forgetting everyday essentials during a busy move.

Tips for printing this dorm packing checklist printable

Print the checklist before shopping and again before packing. Keep one copy with packed boxes so it is easier to find bedding, chargers, documents, and school supplies after arrival.
