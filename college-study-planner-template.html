About this college study planner template

This college study planner template helps college students connect courses, assignments, readings, exams, and study blocks on one printable page. It is useful when a student has lectures, labs, online work, and deadlines spread across several courses.

Use the planner at the start of a college week, before a busy exam period, or after checking each course syllabus. The page keeps study time visible and helps students decide what to handle before the next class meeting.

What is included

  • Course rows for college classes, labs, and online work.

How to use this template

  1. Print one college study planner for the week.
  2. Copy important course dates from your syllabus or learning platform.
  3. Add readings, assignments, and exam preparation tasks.
  4. Block study time before the deadline arrives.
  5. Use the notes section for questions to ask in office hours.

Best for

  • College students managing several courses.
  • Students balancing lectures, labs, and online assignments.
  • Semester planning, midterm review, and final exam prep.
  • Tutors, academic coaches, and college support programs.

Example use case

A college student can use this college study planner template after checking their course portals. They write a psychology reading, a biology lab report, a statistics problem set, and a history quiz review. By adding study blocks before each deadline, the week becomes easier to scan and less dependent on memory.

Printing tips

Print on US Letter or A4 paper and choose fit to page before printing. Keep the page in a binder, homework folder, clipboard, or dry erase sleeve. If the same routine repeats, laminate one copy or use a sleeve with a dry erase marker.
