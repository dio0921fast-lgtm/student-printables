About this printable

A course planner printable helps students understand one class from start to finish. It is useful at the beginning of a semester, when a syllabus is handed out, or whenever a student needs a clean overview of assignments, exams, units, and teacher expectations.

This course planner printable is designed for students who want a practical paper worksheet for course details, units, assignments, exams, teacher notes, and semester planning. It can be used at home, in class, during tutoring, or as part of a binder system. The layout is simple enough to fill in quickly, but structured enough to help students make better decisions about what to do next.

  1. Fill in course and teacher information.

Example use case

A college student can use this course planner after reading a syllabus. The student writes the professor name, materials, major unit topics, paper dates, quiz weeks, and final exam information on one easy reference page.

Printing tips

Print this page on US Letter or A4 paper. Choose fit to page in the print window so the worksheet stays inside the margins. Students can keep a copy in a binder, tape it near a desk, or place it in a dry erase sleeve for repeated use. For classroom use, print several copies and let students choose a fresh page when a new week, unit, project, or exam begins.
