About this exam revision planner template

This exam revision planner template helps students turn exam preparation into clear revision tasks. Instead of writing only the exam date, students can list subjects, topics, practice needs, confidence, time blocks, and completion notes.

The page works before quizzes, midterms, finals, standardized tests, or classroom exams. It is especially helpful when a student knows what to study but needs a printable plan for when and how to review it.

What is included

  • Rows for subjects, topics, revision tasks, and practice work.
  • Time and confidence columns for planning review depth.
  • Done checkbox column for finished tasks.
  • Space for weak areas and last questions.
  • A printable layout that supports exam preparation routines.

How to use this template

  1. Write the exam date and main revision goal.
  2. List every topic that needs review.
  3. Choose a revision task for each topic, such as notes, flashcards, or practice questions.
  4. Add a time block and confidence rating.
  5. Check off finished revision and return to weak areas.

Best for

  • Middle school and high school exam preparation.
  • College midterms, finals, and chapter tests.
  • Tutoring sessions focused on test prep.
  • Homeschool review and teacher-supported revision plans.

Example use case

A student can print this exam revision planner template two weeks before a science test. They list vocabulary, diagrams, formulas, and practice questions as separate rows. After each study session, they mark confidence and check off finished work, then spend the final day on the topics that still feel weak.

Printing tips

Print on US Letter or A4 paper and choose fit to page before printing. Keep the page in a binder, homework folder, clipboard, or dry erase sleeve. If the same routine repeats, laminate one copy or use a sleeve with a dry erase marker.
