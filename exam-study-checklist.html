Who this exam study checklist is for

This exam study checklist is for students who want a simple printable list of review steps before a quiz, test, midterm, or final exam. It is useful for turning vague study plans into concrete actions.

How students can use it

Students can check off tasks such as reviewing notes, reading textbook chapters, practicing sample questions, memorizing key terms, and preparing exam supplies. The small boxes make it easy to mark each task as done while studying.

Tips for printing and reusing it

Print one checklist for each subject or exam. You can also save it as a PDF and print another copy before the next test so the same review routine becomes easier to repeat.
