About this printable

A final exam planner printable helps students prepare for several exams at the same time. It gives each exam a clear place for dates, topics, review tasks, supplies, and final reminders so the last week of school feels less scattered.

This final exam planner printable is designed for students who want a practical paper worksheet for final exam dates, review topics, study tasks, supplies, and last-week preparation. It can be used at home, in class, during tutoring, or as part of a binder system. The layout is simple enough to fill in quickly, but structured enough to help students make better decisions about what to do next.

How to use this template

  1. List every final exam and date.
  2. Add the main topics for each exam.
  3. Choose the next study task for each subject.
  4. Write supplies or materials needed.
  5. Review the planner each night of exam week.

Best for

  • Middle school students building stronger organization habits.
  • High school students balancing homework, tests, and activities.
  • College students who prefer a quick paper planning tool.
  • Homeschool routines, tutoring sessions, and study support.
  • Teachers and parents who want a simple printable page for student planning.

Example use case

A student with finals in math, English, biology, and history can put all exam dates on one page, assign review topics to each class, and make sure calculators, notes, books, and study guides are ready.

Printing tips

Print this page on US Letter or A4 paper. Choose fit to page in the print window so the worksheet stays inside the margins. Students can keep a copy in a binder, tape it near a desk, or place it in a dry erase sleeve for repeated use. For classroom use, print several copies and let students choose a fresh page when a new week, unit, project, or exam begins.
