About this printable

A final exam study schedule helps students plan review before exam week becomes overwhelming. It gives each subject a date, topic, study task, and time block so final review is easier to follow.

Use this final exam study schedule for middle school, high school, college, homeschool, or tutoring. It works well when students have several exams close together and need a paper plan that shows what to study each day.

How to use this template

  1. Write every final exam date first.
  2. List the topics that need review for each subject.
  3. Choose specific study tasks, such as practice problems or note review.
  4. Add realistic time blocks across the week.
  5. Check off completed sessions and adjust the next day if needed.

Best for

  • Middle school students who need clear school organization.
  • High school students balancing assignments, exams, reading, and activities.
  • College students who prefer simple printable planning pages.
  • Homeschool routines, tutoring sessions, and independent study.
  • Teachers and parents who want a practical paper worksheet for student support.

Example use case

A student with finals in math, biology, and English can place the exam dates on the schedule, then assign practice problems, vocabulary review, essay outlining, and note review to different days before the exams.

Printing tips

Print this page on US Letter or A4 paper. Choose fit to page in the print window so the worksheet stays inside the margins. Students can keep a copy in a binder, tape it near a desk, or place it in a dry erase sleeve for repeated use. For classroom use, print several copies and let students choose a fresh page when a new week, unit, project, or exam begins.
