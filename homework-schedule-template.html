About this homework schedule template

A homework schedule template helps students decide when homework will actually happen, not just what homework exists. Many students write assignments down but still struggle because they do not reserve time for each task. This printable creates a simple schedule for homework blocks, breaks, subjects, due dates, and completion.

The homework schedule template works well after school, during study hall, for weekend catch-up, or when several assignments are due close together. It can be printed for one day, one week, or a specific heavy homework period when students need a more visible plan.

This page is intentionally simple so it stays useful after printing. Students can write by hand, teachers can copy it for a class, and parents can keep it in a homework folder or binder.

What is included

  • Time block rows for after-school, evening, and weekend homework.
  • Subject, task, due date, notes, and done columns.
  • A supply checklist area for books, worksheets, devices, and calculators.
  • Space for questions to ask a teacher or tutor.
  • A finished work box so completed assignments are packed for class.
  • A clean printable schedule that works with US Letter and A4 paper.

How to use this template

  1. List all homework tasks before adding times.
  2. Choose realistic homework blocks based on energy, activities, meals, and family routines.
  3. Write the subject, task, and due date into the schedule.
  4. Add short breaks between longer tasks so the plan is easier to follow.
  5. Check off each block after the task is complete or moved to another time.
  6. Use the finished work section before packing your bag for school.

Best for

  • Middle school students building stronger homework and study routines.
  • High school students managing several classes, deadlines, and exams.
  • College students who prefer a printable page beside digital calendars.
  • Homeschool families, tutoring sessions, teachers, and parents.

Example use case

A student has soccer practice after school and three assignments due the next day. They use the homework schedule template to plan a short math block before dinner, reading after dinner, and a science worksheet before packing their bag. The schedule shows where breaks fit so the evening feels less rushed.

The same page can also be reused for tutoring, independent study, class planning, and weekly reset routines. The goal is not to make planning complicated. The goal is to help students see the next action clearly enough to start.

Printing tips

Print on US Letter or A4 paper and choose fit to page before printing. Keep the printable in a binder, clipboard, homework folder, or dry erase sleeve. If the same routine repeats, laminate one copy or place it in a reusable sleeve with a dry erase marker.
