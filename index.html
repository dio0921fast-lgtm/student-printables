Printable study tools

Free student printables for planning, tracking, and studying.

Printable Study is built for students who want simple paper worksheets instead of another app. Each page can be opened in a browser, printed, filled in by hand, or saved as a PDF.

Who these printables are for

The templates work for middle school, high school, college, homeschool, tutoring, and independent study. Students can use them to organize homework, revision, class notes, reading, grades, supplies, and longer projects.

How to choose a template

Start with a weekly study planner if you need an overview of the week, a homework tracker if assignments are the main problem, or a checklist if you want a quick printable routine for exam prep or supplies.

Printing and reusing pages

Most templates are designed for US Letter and A4 paper. Print a fresh copy for each week, class, book, exam, or semester, or save the page as a PDF and keep a reusable digital copy.

Simple layouts

Made for quick printing and writing by hand, with no account or app required.

Student focused

Covers planning, homework, exams, grades, schedules, reading, writing, supplies, and notes.

Easy to expand

New printable pages can be added as separate SEO-friendly HTML templates.
