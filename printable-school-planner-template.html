About this printable school planner template

This printable school planner template gives students one page for the most common school planning details: classes, homework, assignments, reminders, priorities, and study time. It is built for students who want a simple paper planner without opening an app.

The template works well as a weekly school overview, a binder page, or a desk planning sheet. Students can use it before school, after class, during study hall, or at home when preparing for the next day.

What is included

  • A school planning table for classes, homework, assignments, and reminders.
  • Priority and due date columns for better task order.
  • Materials column for books, worksheets, and files.
  • Extra writing space for daily priorities and notes.
  • A clean printable layout for binders and folders.

How to use this template

  1. Print a fresh school planner at the beginning of the week.
  2. Write the main school goal and important dates first.
  3. Add homework, assignments, and reminders as they appear.
  4. Use the priority column to choose what should be done first.
  5. Review the page before packing your school bag.

Best for

  • Students who need one simple school planner page.
  • Middle school and high school homework routines.
  • Tutoring folders and homeschool planning.
  • Teachers and parents supporting organization skills.

Example use case

A student can use this printable school planner template on Sunday evening to write the week ahead. They add a science quiz, two homework tasks, a reading chapter, and a reminder to bring a signed form. During the week, they check the planner before leaving school so fewer tasks are forgotten.

Printing tips

Print on US Letter or A4 paper and choose fit to page before printing. Keep the page in a binder, homework folder, clipboard, or dry erase sleeve. If the same routine repeats, laminate one copy or use a sleeve with a dry erase marker.
