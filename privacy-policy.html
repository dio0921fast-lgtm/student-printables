PrintableStudy provides free printable planners, trackers, checklists, and student worksheet pages. This policy explains what information may be collected when you use this website.

Information we collect

This website does not require accounts, logins, payments, or form submissions to use the printable templates.

If you email us at dio0921fast@gmail.com, we receive the information you choose to include in that email, such as your email address, message, feedback, or template request.

Like most websites, the hosting provider may process basic technical information such as browser type, device information, pages requested, and general traffic data needed to deliver and protect the site.

How information is used

  • To reply to questions, corrections, feedback, or template requests.
  • To maintain, improve, and organize printable student resources.
  • To keep the website working properly and safely.

Cookies and analytics

This site does not currently use accounts or intentionally collect personal information through cookies. If analytics, ads, or other third-party tools are added in the future, this policy should be updated to explain what those services collect.

Children and students

The templates are made for students, parents, and teachers, but the website is not designed to collect personal information from children. Students should ask a parent, guardian, or teacher before sending personal information by email.

Third-party services

This website is published through GitHub Pages. GitHub may process technical information as part of hosting and delivering the site. Please review GitHub's privacy information for details about its own practices.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact dio0921fast@gmail.com.
