Use this free reading log printable to track books, pages, reading time, summaries, vocabulary, and reading goals.

What is included?

  • Book title and author columns
  • Pages read and reading minutes

How to use this reading log

  1. Write the book title and author.
  2. Record pages and minutes after each reading session.
  3. Add a short summary or important idea.
  4. Use the notes area for new words or questions.

Who this reading log printable is for

This reading log printable is for students, parents, and teachers who want a simple way to record daily reading, independent reading, homework reading, or summer reading progress.

How students can use this reading log printable

Students can write the book title, pages read, reading minutes, short notes, and new vocabulary. The log builds a habit of reflecting after each reading session.

Tips for printing this reading log printable

Print several copies for a reading folder or binder. Review completed logs weekly to see reading growth, favorite genres, and books that may need more attention.
