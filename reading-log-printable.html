What is included?

  • Book title and author columns
  • Pages read and reading minutes
  • Summary and vocabulary notes
  • Parent or teacher initials column

How to use this reading log

  1. Write the book title and author.
  2. Record pages and minutes after each reading session.
  3. Add a short summary or important idea.
  4. Use the notes area for new words or questions.

Who this reading log printable is for

This reading log printable is for students who need to track books, pages, reading minutes, and summary notes. It works for classroom reading goals, homework reading, independent reading, and summer reading.

How students can use it

Students can record the date, book title, pages read, minutes spent reading, and a short summary or note. The log helps make reading progress visible over time.

Tips for printing and reusing it

Print one log for each month, book, or reading challenge. Keeping completed logs in a folder can help students prepare for book reports and show consistent reading practice.
