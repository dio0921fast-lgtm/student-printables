Templates for notes, reading, essays, and projects

Reading and writing printables help students capture information, organize ideas, and prepare longer assignments before the final draft. These templates are useful for literature class, research, book reports, essays, presentations, and project planning.

Print a blank copy before class, before reading, or before starting a writing assignment. Completed pages can be kept in a binder and reviewed before tests or due dates.

How reading and writing printables help students

Capture notes while learning

Class notes and Cornell notes templates give students structured space for main ideas, questions, vocabulary, summaries, and follow-up tasks after a lesson or lecture.

Organize reading assignments

Reading logs, book report templates, and vocabulary worksheets help students record what they read, remember key details, and prepare for discussions, quizzes, or writing tasks.

Plan before writing

Essay and project planners help students turn a large assignment into smaller steps, including topic ideas, sources, outlines, deadlines, revisions, and final checks.
