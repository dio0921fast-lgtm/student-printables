Schedule pages for school routines

Printable schedule templates help students see when classes, assignments, tests, and study blocks happen during the week. They are especially helpful when a student has weekend classes, evening sessions, or several deadlines close together.

Use these pages at the start of a term, week, or project period. A printed schedule can live in a binder, locker, study folder, or on a desk.

How printable schedules support school planning

See the full week at once

A printable class schedule helps students map Monday through Sunday, including regular classes, weekend sessions, evening lessons, study blocks, and activity times.

Connect classes and assignments

Schedules work best when paired with homework trackers and assignment calendars. Students can see when a task is due and when they have time to complete it.

Use during a new term

Print a fresh schedule when a semester starts, when classes change, or when exam preparation begins. Keeping a visible schedule can reduce missed classes and forgotten deadlines.

How to choose

Use a planner when the student needs to decide when work will happen, a tracker when progress needs to be recorded, a checklist when the task has repeatable steps, and a schedule when time blocks or class periods matter most.

FAQ

Are these student schedule printables free?

Yes. Open a template to print it, save it from your browser, or download the PDF when available.

Can teachers and parents use these templates?

Yes. Teachers, tutors, homeschool families, parents, and students can print them for school organization.

What paper size should I use?

US Letter and A4 both work well. Choose fit to page before printing.
