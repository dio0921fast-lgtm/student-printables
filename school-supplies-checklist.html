Who this school supplies checklist is for

This school supplies checklist is for students and families preparing for a new school year, semester, or class change. It helps organize common supplies before shopping or packing a backpack.

How students can use it

Students can check off notebooks, pens, pencils, folders, binders, calculators, chargers, and other supplies as they are purchased or packed. The list also works as a quick backpack reset before the first week of school.

Tips for printing and reusing it

Print one copy before school shopping and another copy for the student desk or locker. Reusing the checklist each term helps avoid buying duplicates while still catching missing essentials.
