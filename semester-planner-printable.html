How to use this semester planner

  4. Review the planner every Sunday.

Who this semester planner printable is for

This semester planner printable is for students who want a high-level view of courses, teachers, major due dates, target grades, and important notes for a full term. It is useful at the start of a semester or grading period.

How students can use it

Students can list each course, add teacher information, write major deadlines, and set a target grade or study goal. This helps connect everyday assignments to bigger academic goals.

Tips for printing and reusing it

Print one copy at the beginning of the term and update it when teachers announce projects, exams, or schedule changes. Keep it in the front of a binder for quick reference.
