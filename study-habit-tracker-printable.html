How to use this habit tracker

  1. Choose a few study habits to practice.
  2. Check off each habit every day you complete it.
  3. Review your weekly pattern.
  4. Adjust your study routine for the next week.

Who this study habit tracker printable is for

This study habit tracker printable is for students who want to build consistent routines such as reviewing notes, finishing homework, reading, practicing, or preparing a backpack. It focuses on habits rather than one-time tasks.

How students can use it

Students can choose a few repeatable habits and mark each day they complete them. Over time, the tracker shows which routines are becoming consistent and which ones need more support.

Tips for printing and reusing it

Print one copy per week and keep it somewhere visible. Reusing the same habit tracker for several weeks helps students see progress without needing an app or account.
