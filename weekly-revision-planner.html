About this printable

A weekly revision planner printable is made for students who need to review before tests without cramming everything into one night. It spreads revision across the week so topics, practice questions, notes, and memorization tasks feel more manageable.

This weekly revision planner printable is designed for students who want a practical paper worksheet for revision sessions, subjects, topics, practice tasks, and exam review time. It can be used at home, in class, during tutoring, or as part of a binder system. The layout is simple enough to fill in quickly, but structured enough to help students make better decisions about what to do next.

How to use this template

  1. Write the exam or unit at the top.
  2. Choose one or two topics for each day.
  3. Add a specific revision task instead of writing study.
  4. Set a realistic time block.
  5. Mark each session complete after review.

Best for

  • Middle school students building stronger organization habits.
  • High school students balancing homework, tests, and activities.
  • College students who prefer a quick paper planning tool.
  • Homeschool routines, tutoring sessions, and study support.
  • Teachers and parents who want a simple printable page for student planning.

Example use case

Before a final history exam, a student can plan vocabulary review on Monday, timeline practice on Tuesday, essay outline work on Wednesday, and a short self-quiz on Sunday. This keeps revision visible all week.

Printing tips

Print this page on US Letter or A4 paper. Choose fit to page in the print window so the worksheet stays inside the margins. Students can keep a copy in a binder, tape it near a desk, or place it in a dry erase sleeve for repeated use. For classroom use, print several copies and let students choose a fresh page when a new week, unit, project, or exam begins.
